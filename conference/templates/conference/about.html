ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

In today’s rapidly evolving world...

Objectives

  • To examine how social science research can drive policy reforms and inclusive governance.
  • To explore interdisciplinary collaborations across economics, technology, ethics, and culture.
  • To assess the impact of digitalization and globalization on social justice and equity.
  • To foster knowledge-sharing platforms between scholars, practitioners, and civil society leaders.

WHO CAN BE THE PARTICIPANT

The conference is aimed at providing a meeting ground for the Intellectual and Professional experts...

  • Academicians
  • Policy Makers
  • Research Scholars
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Management Professionals
  • Students

CORE AND SUBTHEMES OF THE CONFERENCE

  1. Society, Equity, Transformation & Social Work: Caste, Class & Gender in the 21st Century...
  2. Knowledge, Education & Human Development: Rethinking Curriculum for the Digital Age...
  3. Politics, Governance, Public Policy, and Political Science: Democratic Values in a Polarized World...
  4. Economy, Technology & Social Impact: Digital Economies and the Future of Work...
  5. Culture, Identity & Globalization: Media Narratives, Cinema, and National Identity...
  6. Environment, Climate & Sustainability: Climate Justice and Environmental Ethics...
  7. Business and Commerce and Business Sustainability: Digital Transformation, e-commerce...
  8. General Social Science and topics not falling under the above categories: Mental Health and Mental Well-being...
