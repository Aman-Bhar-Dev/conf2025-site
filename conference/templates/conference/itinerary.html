Conference Itinerary

A glimpse into the journey, destinations, and memories to be made.

Travel Itinerary

The onward journey to the Bhutan International Conference 2025 begins on 2nd September at 6 AM from New Cooch Behar (West Bengal). All participants will travel together by bus to Thimphu/Paro, Bhutan. The travel experience will be coordinated and guided by Dr. Lhato Jamba. This shared journey is designed to offer participants a chance to connect even before the conference officially begins.

The return journey is scheduled for 6th September at 7 AM, departing from Bhutan and heading back to New Cooch Behar.
