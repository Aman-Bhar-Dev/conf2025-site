Conference Itinerary

A glimpse into the journey, destinations, and memories to be made.

Travel Itinerary

The onward journey to the Bhutan International Conference 2025 begins on 2nd September at 6 AM from New AlipurDuar (WB). All participants will travel together by bus to Thimphu/Paro, Bhutan. The travel experience will be coordinated and guided by Dr. Lhato Jamba. This shared journey is designed to offer participants a chance to connect even before the conference officially begins.

The return journey is scheduled for 6th September at 7 AM, departing from Bhutan and heading back to New Cooch Behar.

How to Reach

Conference Coordinated Journey

Onward Journey: Starts at 6:00 AM, 2 September 2025 from New AlipurDuar (WB).
Participants will travel together by bus to Thimphu/Paro, guided by Dr. Lhato Jamba.

Return Journey: Starts at 7:00 AM, 6 September 2025 for New AlipurDuar (WB).

Self-Mode Journey

By Train: Reach New Alipurduar Jn. (WB) or New Cooch Behar (WB) on 1st September 2025.

By Air: Land at Bagdogra Airport → Transfer to New AlipurDuar (WB) on 1st September 2025 & return from there.

Note: Offline Registration Fee includes Participation in Conference, Conference Kit, Traveling From New AlipurDuar (West Bengal) to Conference Venue and Return, Sightseeing, fooding (MAP Plan), and lodging in Bhutan. *BHUTAN ENTRY TAX SDF Charges may be waived off if the total participants is more than 50. If total offline participants are less than 50, SDF Charges (as applicable) shall be borne by the Participants at the time of entry in Bhutan. * Accompanying person, if any, needs to register separately at the rate of Rs15000/- (For Indian/Bhutan Nationals) and US$ $ 250 (For Foreign Nationals). Date of Journey from New AlipurDuar (West Bengal) to Bhutan: 2nd September 2025, 6 am, Return reach New AlipurDuar (West Bengal) from Bhutan on 6th September, 2025, by evening. Dr Lhato Jamba, Local Organising Secretary, shall receive the participant and act as a guide for transportation to Bhutan.

Note: All participants registered for Offline mode must travel in a group.
